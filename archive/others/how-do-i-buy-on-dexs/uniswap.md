# Uniswap

01\. Go to: https://app.uniswap.org/

02\. Put USDt as the first currency of exchange.&#x20;

03\. WARNING The network must be chosen - Mainnet Ethereum and no other!

04\. After that, select NIFTSY token as second currency - preferably immediately via smart contract address: 0x7728cd70b3dd86210e2bd321437f448231b81733 (you can check it in our documentation):

![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/Снимок экрана 2022-08-04 в 10.05.54.png>)

05\. You then need to give permission to exchange tokens and go to Metamask or another Uniswap-connected wallet:

![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/Снимок экрана 2022-08-04 в 10.09.39.png>)

06\. You can also import tokens into MetaMask or another wallet by adding the NIFTSY smart contract address: 0x7728cd70b3dd86210e2bd321437f448231b81733:

![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/Снимок экрана 2022-08-04 в 10.12.54.png>)
